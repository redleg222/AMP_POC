The Art of the Story

By Shay Anderson

Essential Elements

Five elements keep a story running smoothly and allow the action to develop in a logical way

Characters

The characters are the individuals that the story is about and should be introduced with enough information that the reader can visualize each person

Setting

The setting is the location of the action and should describe the surroundings in such detail that the reader can picture the scene

Plot

The plot is the actual story which all action is based and needs a clear beginning, middle, and end so that the reader can make sense of the action

Conflict

Every story has a conflict to solve and the plot is centered on this conflict and the ways in which the characters attempt to resolve the problem

Resolution

The solution to the problem is the way the action is resolved and it is important that the resolution fits the rest of the story in tone and creativity
